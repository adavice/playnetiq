Privacy Policy

We use your data for clear reasons, keep it safe, and store it just as long as needed. We’re transparent about how everything is handled.

Strict measures are taken to ensure that your personal information is safe from loss, misuse, and unauthorized access. PCI-compliant encryption is used in payment transactions. We have procedures in place to react swiftly and reduce any dangers, even though no system is impenetrable.

Data Handling and Compliance

To fulfill our agreement, we collect and use your personal data for payment processing, order verification, subscription management, updates, and requested offers. Your information also supports our business operations, including sharing within the playnetiq.com group, preventing misuse, aiding corporate actions, targeted marketing (with consent), and analysis. Marketing messages like newsletters and promotions are sent only if you agree. We comply with legal obligations by processing data as required.

Compliance with Denmark’s data and digital regulations alongside the GDPR (EU 2016/679) guides how we manage and protect your personal information responsibly.

Your personal data might be stored or processed outside the European Economic Area, including in countries without an official adequacy decision. Within the playnetiq.com group, employees or service providers abroad may handle your orders, payments, or support. We apply strict safeguards to protect your data and comply with this Privacy Policy.

Information We Need to Serve You

When you use our service, you provide certain required information (marked with *) like contact details, login credentials, or payment data is provided by you. Other details, such as your IP address or interactions with content and ads, are gathered automatically through cookies and tracking tools.

Logging in with your email, password, or social media account grants access to playnetiq.com and its related community sites. Each site requires agreement to its own terms, privacy, and cookie policies. Your login details verify your subscription and identity and are kept confidential within playnetiq.com.

To process payments and personalize your experience, we may use data from partners like payment providers or related platforms (e.g., playnetiq.com).

Reasons for Sharing Personal Data

Your data may be shared within our group for service and administrative needs, and with your consent, for marketing. Participating in surveys or contests could share your info with partners, while non-personal aggregated data may be shared with third parties to improve services.

To comply with laws, protect rights, prevent fraud, handle disputes, and assess credit, your information might also be disclosed to authorized parties, all of whom must follow this privacy policy.

Your Data Rights

Your rights include accessing, changing, deleting, or limiting your data, objecting to its use, and requesting portability. Complaints can be filed with authorities if needed. Consent withdrawal won’t affect prior processing.
